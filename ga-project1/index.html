Using Word Connoisseur

Word Connoisseur is a site that let's you put together your own word lists without the hassle of typing out each word and definition as with many flash card sites. Simply type the word you're looking for in the search box and press 'enter'. The word and definition will appear below.

If you'd like to add this word to your practice list, click on the word. This will add the word to your list and create a flashcard below.
