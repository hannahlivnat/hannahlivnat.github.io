Using Word Connoisseur

Word Connoisseur is a site that let's you put together word lists without hassle. Type the word you're looking for in the search box and press 'enter'. The word and definition will appear below. If you'd like to add this word to your practice list, click on the 'add button'. This will add the word to your list and create a flashcard below.
